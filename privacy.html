Privacy Policy

Detekta is designed to work fully offline and respects your privacy.

Detekta does not collect, transmit, store, or sell personal data. All data you create in the app — including surveys, targets, finds, photos, GPS locations, and tracks — is stored locally on your device only.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

Location Access

Detekta may request access to your device’s location while the app is in use to support mapping, navigation, and track recording features. Location data is never transmitted off your device.

Camera Access

Camera access is used only when you choose to take photos of surveys, targets, or finds. Photos remain stored locally unless you export them.

Data Export

You may export your data manually in formats such as GPX, JSON, ZIP, and photo galleries. Exported files are fully under your control.

Disclaimer

Detekta is provided as a field logging and navigation aid for metal detectorists. The developer makes no guarantees regarding accuracy of GPS data, location precision, or suitability for any specific purpose.

Users are responsible for ensuring they comply with all local laws, land access permissions, and detecting regulations when using the app.
